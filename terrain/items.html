Outdoor items, as the name implies, are items that are best suited to be used outdoors. Though they can also be placed inside houses or dens, some of them can take up a lot of space due to their size.

Items under this category have varied functionality so be sure to check each of them out. Some may also be required to create a Habitat and unlock a new Pokemon!
